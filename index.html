Andrew Ferraiuolo

I am a computer scientist and software engineer. Usually I work on problems in security and privacy, though my work intersects with programming languages (formal verification, logic programming, type systems, compilers, static analysis, and language design), computer hardware (computer architecture, electronic design automation, and hardware implementation) and computer systems (operating systems, blockchain). I do both experiment-driven systems-building research as well as theoretical work. I have published research in top-tier venues for all of these domains, and brought research to industry-scale production at Google. I have a PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Cornell University.
